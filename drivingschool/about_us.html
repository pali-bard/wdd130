From The Beginning

Since 2002, The Driving School has provided Tooele County students with a safe and educational environment to complete their Driver's Education requirement for their Utah state driver's license.
In January 2019, The Driving School opened it's doors under new ownership with a brand new staff. Our team remains committed to providing the best and safest educational Driver Education experience for Tooele County students.
